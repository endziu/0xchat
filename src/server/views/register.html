One-time setup

To send and receive encrypted messages, connect your wallet and sign once. This derives your encryption key and registers your public key on the server. No gas needed — it's just a signature.

Connected:
Privacy note: Your encryption public key (not your private key) is stored on the server. The derivation is deterministic — signing the same message always produces the same key. Nobody can read your messages without your wallet signature.
